# Deepen Plan

## Inputs

- PRD and constraints.
- Known dependencies.
- Scope options.

## Outputs

- `docs/shape/<slug>/plan.md`.

## Steps

1. Break work into steps and dependencies.
2. Identify riskiest step and mitigation.
3. Define cut lines for scope control.

## Verification

- Plan includes sequencing, dependencies, and cut lines.
