# Trash

## Inputs

- Target file paths.
- Desired action (delete or archive).
- Reason for removal.

## Outputs

- Removed files or archived copies.
- Notes about what was removed.

## Steps

1. Confirm targets and reason.
2. Prefer archival when risk is unclear.
3. Record removals in notes or changelog.

## Verification

- Removal list and confirmation recorded.
