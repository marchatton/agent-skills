# Pricing and Packaging

## Inputs

- Target segment.
- Value metrics.
- Competitive pricing context.

## Outputs

- Candidate pricing/packaging options.
- Recommended option with risks.

## Steps

1. Define value metric and willingness signals.
2. Draft 2-3 pricing/packaging options.
3. Choose a recommended option with risks.

## Verification

- Recommendation includes rationale and known risks.
