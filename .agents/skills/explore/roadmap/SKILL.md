# Roadmap

## Inputs

- Strategic goals.
- Known dependencies.
- Resource constraints.

## Outputs

- High-level roadmap with milestones.
- Next milestone definition.

## Steps

1. List goals and constraints.
2. Sequence milestones by dependency and impact.
3. Define the next milestone and timing assumption.

## Verification

- Milestones and next milestone are explicit.
